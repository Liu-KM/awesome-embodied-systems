# Focus on systems for embodied foundation models

The catalog will focus on systems whose primary workload is a VLA, WAM, embodied VLM/LLM, or infrastructure designed specifically for such models. Autonomous-driving-only systems, traditional robotics, generic middleware and ROS components, classical simulators, teleoperation platforms, and general robot-learning frameworks remain out of scope even when they are excellent systems work.

This narrower boundary sacrifices breadth for a coherent research object: infrastructure shaped by the training, inference, deployment, simulation, evaluation, and reliability demands of embodied foundation models. Model-system co-design remains eligible, but incidental use of a large model does not.
