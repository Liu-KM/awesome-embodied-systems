# Require recognition for systems older than 12 months

After a candidate passes the first two gates and the technical-evidence part of the third, apply the rolling maturity check. A system more than 12 months past its first verifiable public release must have at least 20 citations, a formal peer-reviewed archival conference or journal publication, or 100 stars on its official GitHub repository; otherwise it is archived from the public catalog.

Newer systems may enter provisionally because systems research often appears before publication and community adoption can be measured. Citation and star counts must record their source and check date, and provisional entries are rechecked once they become more than 12 months old.
